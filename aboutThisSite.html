About this Site


      This site began in the summer of 2017 as a database of Ryan Cosner's projects over the years, documenting various large projects that he has worked on. It serves as a scrapbook, "Maker's Portfolio", and a digital resume as well.

      Although website building has become easier and easier over the years with a variety of templates and services which can provide you with a more or less pre-built site, Ryan Cosner, driven by an interest in understanding everything at a foundational level, decided to build this site from the ground-up. More information about building this website can be found here: Site Build Project .
